# Lung Nodule AI Diagnostic System - Design DNA

This document serves as the source of truth for the visual identity and UI patterns of the Lung Nodule AI Diagnostic System, following the Stitch design methodology.

## 🎨 Color Palette
- **Brand Primary**: `#0284c7` (Sky 600) - Used for primary actions and highlights.
- **Brand Secondary**: `#0f172a` (Slate 900) - Used for sidebar and secondary panels.
- **Background**: `#020617` (Slate 950) - The main background of the application.
- **Surface**: `#1e293b` (Slate 800) - Used for cards and elevated surfaces.
- **Text Primary**: `#f8fafc` (Slate 50) - Main readable text.
- **Text Secondary**: `#94a3b8` (Slate 400) - Descriptions and labels.
- **Success**: `#10b981` (Emerald 500)
- **Warning**: `#f59e0b` (Amber 500)
- **Danger**: `#ef4444` (Red 500)

## ✍️ Typography
- **Primary Font**: `Inter`, `Segoe UI`, `sans-serif`
- **Heading 1**: 24px, Extra Bold (900), tracking 1px
- **Heading 2**: 18px, Bold (700)
- **Body**: 14px, Regular (400)
- **Caption**: 11px, Medium (500), uppercase, tracking 1px

## 📐 Layout & Spacing
- **Base Unit**: 8px
- **Border Radius**: 12px for cards, 8px for buttons.
- **Margins**: 20px - 30px between major sections.
- **Structure**:
  - **Sidebar**: Fixed 260px width, Slate 900 background.
  - **Main Area**: Flexible, Slate 950 background.
  - **Cards**: Surface color, 1px border (Slate 800), rounded 12px.

## ✨ Components
- **Buttons**:
  - **Primary**: Gradient Sky 600 to Blue 600, Bold text, center-aligned.
  - **Ghost/Side**: Transparent background, Slate 400 text, left-aligned, icons.
- **Cards**: Subtle 1px border, padded with 20px inner spacing.
- **Inputs**: Slate 800 background, Slate 700 border, blue focus ring.
